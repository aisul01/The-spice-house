Meet Our Chef

Julian Vance

Born in the Pacific Northwest and trained in the rigorous kitchens of Paris, Chef Julian Vance blends classical French technique with hyper-local, foraged ingredients. Known for his minimalist presentation and obsession with fermentation, Julian spent a decade helming three-Michelin-starred kitchens before launching Acre—a conceptual dining experience dedicated to zero-waste gastronomy. When he isn't in the kitchen, he can be found documenting the intersection of food anthropology and micro-climatology.
